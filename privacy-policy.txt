Privacy Policy for "Professional Exam Tests in Russian" Extension

1. Data Collection
Our extension does not collect any personal identifiable information such as name, email, address, or payment details. The extension works by translating content from the website testy.egzaminzawodowy.info to Russian and removing advertisements. All translations happen locally in the browser, and no data is sent to external servers beyond what is necessary for translation purposes.

2. Website Interaction
The extension modifies the content of testy.egzaminzawodowy.info by adding translations and removing ads. It does not track, store, or share your activity on this or any other websites.

3. Cookies
The extension itself does not create, store, or manipulate cookies. However, it interacts with cookies already present on testy.egzaminzawodowy.info, which are used by the site for its functionality. The extension does not access, modify, or share cookies for any external purpose.

4. External API Usage
To provide translations, the extension connects to egzamin.webscrapp.rocks, which delivers the translated content. No personal data is sent or received during this process.

5. Third-party Access
We do not sell, share, or provide any user data to third-party companies or services.

6. Data Security
We prioritize the security of your data by limiting all interactions and processing to your browser. The extension does not store any user data.

7. Changes to this Policy
Any future updates or changes to this privacy policy will be reflected here. We encourage you to review this page periodically for the latest information.

8. Contact Information
If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, feel free to contact us at pavelgeveiler@gmail.com.
